About Us

Learn More About ESS System

Welcome to ESS!, a Exam Schedule System for colleges and universities to schedule their organization's timetable. TTGS help colleges to generate their timetable with ease and maximum efficiency and saving their valuable time.

Mission

our mission is to provide a simple and user-friendly platform for scheduling exams and creating innovative solutions that help college to save time, reduce errors, and streamline their exam management process.

How does it work?

Exam Schedule System makes use of a Genetic Algorithm that helps generate the most optimal and suited timetable for their colleges needs.
